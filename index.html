Welcome to the introduction of my IxD project, focused on gamifying a learning app. My aim is to make the learning experience more engaging and interactive for our users.

Gamification applies game design principles to non-game contexts, like education. The objective is to increase user motivation, engagement, and enjoyment.

This project involved various phases, including a competitive analysis, wireframing, prototyping, and usability testing.

The competitive analysis helped to indentify strengths and weaknesses in the existing market, and highlight areas we can improve.

Wireframing and prototyping allowed us to design and develop the interface and user experience of the app, ensuring a smooth and enjoyable learning process.

Finally, usability testing was conducted to gather feedback from users on the app's usability and functionality. This was used to refine and improve the app to meet user needs.

Overall, the aim is to create a learning app that is not only effective but also enjoyable, ultimately improving the user's learning outcomes.
